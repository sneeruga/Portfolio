Overview

Charlie is an intelligent personal navigation assistant with three distinct driver modes that you can take with you anywhere.

Current navigation applications don’t adapt well to changes. For example, if a driver chooses to deviate from a route, he/she will receive insufficient feedback or cryptic, unhelpful commands. Communicating intentions during a trip is difficult. Often it results in drivers needing to fumble with a touchscreen while driving which is dangerous. Information exchange loops are inadequate and existing navigation improvements don’t focus enough on driver-device interactivity.

Our team wanted to augment the multimodal capabilities of navigation applications we built an implicitly inferring driving behavior providing actionable feedback, adapting intelligently to changes, and empowering drivers to make safer decisions.
