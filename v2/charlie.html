Charlie: Personal Navigation Assistant

Overview

Charlie is an intelligent personal navigation assistant with three distinct driver modes that you can take with you anywhere.

Problem Space

1. Current navigation applications don’t adapt well to changes. For example, if a driver chooses to deviate from a route, he/she will receive insufficient feedback or cryptic, unhelpful commands.

2. Communicating intentions during a trip is difficult. Often it results in drivers needing to fumble with a touchscreen while driving which is dangerous.

3. Information exchange loops are inadequate and existing navigation improvements don’t focus enough on driver-device interactivity.

Our team wanted to augment the multimodal capabilities of navigation applications we built an implicitly inferring driving behavior providing actionable feedback, adapting intelligently to changes, and empowering drivers to make safer decisions.

3 Driving Modes

1

Guided Mode

Charlie uses conversational language and a customized language set so that you can be prepared to follow directions in a simple and safe manner. A holographic visual display will highlight the path you need to take to avoid any confusion you might have.

2

Learning Mode

Charlie can follow you as you drive and learn your preferences from the preferred routes you take as well as your driving habits. Charlie will remain quiet as you drive making sure you are fully focused on the road to get safely to your destination.

3

Assisted Mode

Charlie can give control to your friends and family in the car to help you navigate. Audio output will be turned off so you can converse freely with passengers but visual guidance will be provided so you won’t miss any directions. Learning mode can also be incorporated during this mode.
